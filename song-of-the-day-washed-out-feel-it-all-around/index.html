Song of the Day: Washed Out - "Feel it all around"

Jan 13, 2012 By Adarsh Pandit in SOTD

New favorite song/band discovered as the theme song for Fred Armisen and Carrie Brownstein’s “Portlandia” (New Yorker Review of Season 2), which is a hipster show which makes fun of hipsters. Also, it’s funny.

I’m a sucker for sentimental music, which this certainly is. It reminds me of Boards of Canada in a way (Great retro video for “ROYGBIV”). Or the more obscure “Casino vs. Japan” (their amazing “Aquarium” (youtube)

Anyhow, enjoy. (Buy from Amazon)
